Every year, Extremely Shy- Looking for Friends hosts 2 events that are a cut above the rest, and at least for me I can say are the highlights of my year. One of those events is our New Years party and the other is our Halloween Party that is now just a few days away! This Friday, join us at the Bourbon for drinks, dancing, awesome people and a ton of fun games and activities. I’m sending out this email to let you guys know that tomorrow will be your last opportunity to get your tickets at the early bird price of $15 so if you’ve been considering attending, please don’t miss this opportunity to buy your tickets at the early bird price. And if haven’t been considering attending then for the love of god why not!?

Here’s a link to the event with more details: https://www.meetup.com/Extremely-Shy-Looking-for-friends/events/241777345/

And a link to the payment page here: https://www.meetup.com/Extremely-Shy-Looking-for-friends/events/243641872/

This is going to be one of the best and largest meetup events in Vancouver all year and i assure you that you don’t want to miss it! Some things you can expect at this event.

  • Tons of great prizes given away in icebreaker activities to best costume winners, and randomly drawn from door tickets.
  • Lots of fun games to keep you busy if you’re like me and don’t like to spend the whole night dancing like Boccee ball, beer pong, pool, giant jenga and more.
  • All of your meetup friends gathered together in one place for a great night of drinks dancing and awesome conversations.
